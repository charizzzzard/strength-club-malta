05 — GOOD TO KNOW

Frequently asked questions.

Is there a staffed reception desk?

A staffed reception desk is not planned. The club is intended to run digitally, while cleaning, maintenance, support and operational responsibility remain part of the model. The final setup will be published before opening.

What if the club is busy when I arrive?

Managing membership relative to the floor is central to the model. We will not promise that every piece of equipment is available at every moment, but the club is deliberately being designed not to maximise membership at the expense of the training experience.

Where in Malta will the club be?

The location has not been confirmed. The building will shape the final floor plan, and the location will be announced once an agreement is in place.

Will member access be available around the clock?

Round-the-clock member access is planned, but feasibility depends on the final site, permissions and operating setup. The confirmed access hours will be published before opening.

What will membership cost?

The current pricing plan is €49 per month for founding members, with a planned standard membership rate of €69 per month and a €15 day pass. These prices form part of the current business model; final membership terms will be confirmed before opening.

Who is Strength Club for?

Anyone who enjoys training and is motivated to work on themselves. You do not need to be advanced. The club is intended for people who value flexible access, a focused environment, good equipment and a floor that is managed responsibly.
